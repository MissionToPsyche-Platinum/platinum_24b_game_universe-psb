A digital version of the "Physche Against the Universe" card game created by Nihah Shah
as part of the Physche Inspire Program.

The game aims to meet the intent of the original and provides a digital means of learning about the 
Psyshce Mission as well as having fun.

Sponsored by: Cassie Bowman, Arizona State Univerity through a partnership with NASA. Capstone Project for
Penn State University, SWENG 480 and 481.
