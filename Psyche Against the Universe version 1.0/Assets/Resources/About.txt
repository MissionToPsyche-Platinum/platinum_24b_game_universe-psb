A digital version of the "Psyche Against the Universe" card game created by Nihah Shah as part of the Psyche Inspire Program.

The game aims to meet the intent of the original and provides a digital means of learning about the Psyche Mission as well as having fun.

Sponsored by: Cassie Bowman, Arizona State Univerity through a partnership with NASA. Capstone Project for Penn State University, SWENG 480 and 481.
