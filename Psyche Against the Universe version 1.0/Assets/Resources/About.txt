A digital version of the "Psyche Against the Universe" card game created by Nihah Shah as part of the Psyche Inspire Program.

The game aims to meet the intent of the original and provides a digital means of learning about the Psyche Mission as well as having fun.

This work was created in partial fulfillment of Pennsylvania State University Capstone Course "SWENG 480/481". 
The work is a result of the Psyche Student Collaborations component of NASA’s Psyche Mission (https://psyche.ssl.berkeley.edu).
“Psyche: A Journey to a Metal World” [Contract number NNM16AA09C] is part of the NASA Discovery Program mission to solar system targets.
